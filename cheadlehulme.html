CHEADLE HULME





Bruntwood Park




Do you live in Cheadle Hulme? If so, I recommend you visit Bruntwood Park. Bruntwood Park is one of the biggest parks in Cheadle Hulme. There are enormous activities you can do like, pitch and putt, BMX track, ducks, pigeon, and other birds feeding. They have a kid’s playground with seesaw, slides, beach sand, and a basketball ring. It is a park that you can stay for a holiday, as the parks is very closed with hotels. If you feel hungry and need a place to drink the place have a vinery. If you like a more peaceful area there is a huge open field to walk. The place has parking place. For more information and assurance visit Bruntwood Park google map satellite view and location.



South Park




South Park is a small park and is great place to stay after a rough day from work or studies. It has a peaceful atmosphere as it is surrounded with trees. The place has two basketball ring one is for junior and the other one is for senior, and small skate park. They also have a kid’s playground so if you are that person who likes slides and swings. What makes is great is the lovely beaches to seat on. The place is near local stores, so you feel hungry or thirsty the place very casual. Click here to visit South Park location.



Hesketh Park




Hesketh Park is a very small park that doesn’t have any kind of activities. However, the park is great for relaxations, and they have benches to sit on. The park is surrounded with trees which creates a cool sensation and feeling of restfulness. It is close from Cheadle Hulme town. Click here to visit Hesketh Park location.
